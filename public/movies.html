The Best Movies According to Kashish.

My top 3 movies of all time

Kabhie Khushi Kabhi Ghum

Loving,family film with a perfect blend of humour,emotions and romance.

Titanic

Highlights the undying spirit of hope and love in the face of an unexpected tragedy.

Pride and Prejudice 2005

The best adaptation of my most favourite book of all time by Jane Austen.
The cinematography is aesthetically pleasing and the chemistry and romance between the protagonists is almost tangible.
